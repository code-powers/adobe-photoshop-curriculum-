CopyrightLegal protection automatically given to original creative works the moment they're created. Includes photos, art, music, writing, and other creative work. = Legal protection for creative work. Think of it like a "Do Not Touch" sign on someone's art.

The Rule: If someone created it, they own it. You can't use it without permission (unless it's free or you buy a licenseA legal agreement that gives you permission to use someone's copyrighted work under specific conditions.).
üö® Reality Check: Using the wrong image can cost $150,000 in fines! That's not a joke - it's real lawsuits.

What's Protected by Copyright:

  • Photos (even phone pics)
  • Artwork and illustrations
  • Logo designs
  • Music and videos

üÜò Need Help?

Quick Answers:

Q: Can I use any image from Google?
A: NO! Google Images just shows you images from around the web - they're not free to use.
Q: What's a license?
A: A license is like a permission slip that tells you how you can use someone's creative work.
Q: Why do I need to worry about copyright?
A: Because using the wrong image can result in huge fines ($150,000+) and lawsuits.
